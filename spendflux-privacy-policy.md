# Privacy Policy

**Effective date: June 2025**

SpendFlux ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal finance tracking application. This policy explains how we handle your data.

---

## Data Collection

SpendFlux does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal or financial data on external servers. All data you enter — transactions, accounts, budgets, categories, and tags — is stored **exclusively on your device** in a local database.

---

## Google Sign-In

We use Google Sign-In solely to display your name, email address, and profile photo within the app. This information is stored locally on your device and is never shared with any third party.

---

## Google Drive Backup

If you choose to use the backup feature, your data is uploaded directly to **your own Google Drive account**. We have no access to your Drive or your backup files. This feature is entirely optional.

---

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party, advertiser, or analytics provider. The app contains no analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking libraries.

---

## Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling SpendFlux. To remove Drive backups, delete the "SpendFlux Backups" folder from your Google Drive.
