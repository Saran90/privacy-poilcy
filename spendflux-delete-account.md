# SpendFlux — Account & Data Deletion Request

**App:** SpendFlux
**Developer:** SpendFlux

---

## How to Delete Your Account and Data

Since SpendFlux stores all data locally on your device, you are in full control of your data at all times. Follow the steps below to delete your account and associated data.

---

## Step 1 — Sign Out of Your Google Account

1. Open SpendFlux
2. Tap the **Profile** tab at the bottom
3. Scroll down and tap **Sign Out**

This removes your Google account credentials (name, email, profile photo) from the app.

---

## Step 2 — Delete All Local App Data

Uninstall SpendFlux from your device. This permanently deletes the local database containing all your transactions, accounts, budgets, categories, and tags.

---

## Step 3 — Delete Google Drive Backups (if applicable)

If you used the Google Drive backup feature:

1. Open [Google Drive](https://drive.google.com)
2. Locate the folder named **SpendFlux Backups**
3. Right-click and select **Move to Trash**
4. Empty your Google Drive Trash

---

## What Data is Deleted

| Data Type | Deleted When |
|-----------|-------------|
| Name, email, profile photo (Google Sign-In) | Step 1 — Sign Out |
| Transactions, accounts, budgets, categories, tags | Step 2 — Uninstall |
| Google Drive backup files | Step 3 — Delete Drive folder |

## What Data is Retained

SpendFlux does not operate any servers or databases. No data is retained by us after completing the steps above.
